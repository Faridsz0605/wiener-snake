# Wiener Snake AI — Narration Script

**Format:** LinkedIn short-form | ~60 seconds | Voice-over narration
**Tone:** Professional, sober, confident. No hype.

---

## Scene 1 — (0:00 - 0:06)

What if you could teach an AI to play a game — without ever telling it the rules?

---

## Scene 2 — (0:06 - 0:14)

This is Snake. You have probably played it before. But this time, no one is at the controls.

---

## Scene 3 — (0:14 - 0:24)

The agent does not see the screen the way we do. It receives eleven signals. Where is the danger. Which direction am I moving. Where is the food. That is its entire world.

---

## Scene 4 — (0:24 - 0:38)

Those signals feed into a neural network. Eleven inputs, one hidden layer, three possible actions: go straight, turn right, or turn left. Every time it eats, it is rewarded. Every time it crashes, it is penalized. Over time, it figures out what works.

---

## Scene 5 — (0:38 - 0:52)

At first, it moves randomly. It dies in seconds. But game after game, the scores start climbing. By game three hundred, it is navigating with intent. By five hundred, it rarely makes a mistake.

---

## Scene 6 — (0:52 - 1:00)

No rules were written. No paths were hardcoded. Just a reward signal and a network that learned what to do with it. Built from scratch with Python and PyTorch.
